Team Rocketry comprises 40 dedicated students from DTU, chosen through a rigorous selection process, bound together by their collective enthusiasm for space exploration. The team is led by experienced senior members who also oversee other university tech teams involved in projects like model Mars rovers, CanSat satellites, and automobile manufacturing. Team Rocketry stands out in India as one of the rare groups dedicated to constructing model rockets. Their primary goal is to clinch victory at the prestigious Spaceport America Cup 2024, the largest international rocketry competition among colleges. Their passion and innovation drive them to pursue excellence in the realm of spaceflight, making them a standout force in the world of student-led aerospace endeavors.

Avionics and GNC

The heart of our rocket's technology lies within its sophisticated electronics system, a marvel of modern engineering. This cutting-edge system features a state-of-the-art GPS module, meticulously designed to flawlessly monitor and precisely track the rocket's position as it hurtles towards the heavens.

Propulsion

Embarking on an awe-inspiring journey into the skies, we have set our sights on designing and crafting a formidable Solid Rocket Motor (SRM) that will propel us to new heights, quite literally! Our mission is nothing short of exhilarating: to soar to an impressive altitude of 10,000 feet, all while harnessing a total impulse range between 9,000 and 12,000 Newton-seconds.

Recovery

In our quest for reusability and cost-effectiveness, we've harnessed the ingenuity of our recovery system. At the heart of this innovative design, we've harnessed the power of parachutes. Not just one, but a dynamic duo, featuring a Drogue parachute to gracefully slow the descent, followed by the sturdy embrace of the Main parachute for a gentle touchdown.

Aerostructures

Our focus is on airframe design, analysis, and manufacturing. We adopt a passive control strategy, keeping the fins aligned for the entire flight, ensuring stability. Rigorous simulations encompass every element of our rocket, from aerodynamics to launch dynamics. Our mission is to create a rocket that's not just a vessel but a masterpiece, reflecting our unwavering dedication to reaching the stars.

Business

At the helm of our mission, we have a multifaceted leader, the guardian of our team's success. Firstly, they are the architect of powerful partnerships, forging connections with potential sponsors and suppliers that fuel our dreams. But that's just the beginning; they're also the curator of our team's brand image, ensuring it shines brightly in the vast cosmos of innovation.
