Ad Astera Per Aspera

Who Are We

We are a group of 40 highly motivated students, driven by a vision of achieving excellence in space exploration. Their mission involves participating in esteemed rocketry competitions and engaging in various aerospace-related projects, all underpinned by their collective enthusiasm and passion for space exploration.

Our Vision

We aspire to excel in the domain of space exploration, epitomizing innovation and dedication, and making their mark on the global stage of aerospace endeavors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to achieve victory at the renowned Spaceport America Cup 2024, the largest international rocketry competition among colleges. We are actively involved in various tech projects related to space, including model Mars rovers, CanSat satellites, and automobile manufacturing. Our collective enthusiasm, passion, and unwavering dedication drive us towards excellence in spaceflight, establishing themselves as a remarkable force in the world of student-led aerospace endeavors.
