Ad Astera Per Aspera

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower minds and reach new heights by fostering a passion for rocketry and aerospace. We aim to achieve an altitude of 10,000 feet and ultimately embark on a space shot, pioneering the frontiers of what is possible in the field of model rocketry. Through innovation, dedication, and teamwork, we build our own rocket engines and rocket bodies, pushing the boundaries of aerospace technology.

Our Vision

We are driven by a vision to inspire the future of aerospace by creating a world where the excitement, knowledge, and possibilities of rocketry are accessible to everyone. We envision a society where space exploration is not limited to a select few but becomes a shared dream that unites people from all walks of life.
