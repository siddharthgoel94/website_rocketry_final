Meet Our Team

Hey! Team Rocketry comprises 40 meticulously selected DTU students, united by a shared passion for space exploration. Guided by seasoned leaders overseeing various tech projects, including model Mars rovers, CanSat satellites, and automobile manufacturing. Noteworthy in India, their paramount objective is triumph at the prestigious Spaceport America Cup 2024, the world's largest intercollegiate rocketry competition. Fueled by boundless passion and innovation, they drive towards excellence in aerospace, emerging as a formidable presence in the realm of student-led endeavors.
